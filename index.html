F.A.Q

  • Can any indiviual can participate in the contest or we need to register as a team ?

    No, indivuals can't register. They should and must be a team of 3 members minimun and 4 members maximum. 3 members will be permitted on podium, participants will decide who'll answering 1 can be accessed to replace if required.

  • Is the compitation will be held Online or Offline

    3 (Three) qualifying rounds will be conducted through online and top 9 (Nine) selected teams need to arrive at Hyderabad, Telangana,India for Quater, Semi and Finals of the Championship.

  • How is the team registration process done ?

    Participants need to pay the registration fees of $99 USD through online by sharing the Name, Age, Profession and E-mail address. The registration charges includes the charges for 3 qualifying rounds fees via online and also the model questions booklet which will be shared with the user via email post registration fees is done.

  • How is the qualifying rounds will be conducted and how teams will be Qualified?

    Once the teams registration process is completed the emails will be send of date and time. Every team need to undergo all 3 rounds which will be conducted online. The Final teams will be selected on the Scored marks, quick timing and other performance parameters.

  • May i know what type of qualification round ? And how its outline of the rounds ?

    All 3 rounds are manditory to be attended by the registered teams and teams will be qualified based on top scored
    Round-1 100 Marks for the duration of 120 minutes with No Negative Marking
    Round-2 100 Marks for the duration of 120 minutes with Negative Marking
    Round-3 100 Marks for the duration of 120 minutes with Negative Marking

  • Is the qualified teams will need to make there own arrangements to visit Hyderabadif qualified ?

    Yes, qualified teams need to make there own arrangements to visit Hyderabad, India. Which includes Travel and VISA charges. We'll help the selected teams by sharing the letter which can be submitted to apply for the VISA to visit India. And stay will be arranged from the organisors at Hyderabad which will be near to the venue.

  • Do we get any local support post arriving at Hyderabad for the Quiz ?

    Only for qualified teams which arrives at Hyderabad will be provided the sharing rooms with food and other snacks from the organisors. Pick and drop towards the venue will be arranged. For complete event post getting disqualified from the shoot out rounds. They teams will be permitted to stay for entire event. Post the event is completed they need to make the personal arrangements.
